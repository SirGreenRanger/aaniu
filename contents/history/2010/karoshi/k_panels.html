Panels and their Descriptions:

Baka Desnay! (Being An Idiot In Japan) - Want to know what it's like to learn Japanese, live in Japan, and then start a crazy-amazing indie animation project to teach Japanese? Then this panel is for you. Jimmy Trevor (known online as Sonicsuns) lived in Japan for 7 months, and started a blog called The Man In Japan. He is also starting up an animation project called "Baka Desnay!", which teaches Japanese in the most awesome way you can imagine. Jimmy will discuss his various experiences and plans, and will take questions from the audience. (Oh, and he's looking for volunteers for that animation project!)

Pachinko - Pachinko is a mechanical game like pinball played in arcades all across Japan. Come learn a little about the game and take a turn playing some of them.

Prove me Wrong - This is a discussion panel where the audience attempts to defend anime topics that we don't like. A group of friends with different tastes in anime pick out major topics, present a brief case on why they think is sucks, and let a few people from the audience give their rebuttal. Good rebuttals can be rewarded with a treat.

"The World" A .hack// panel - A fan of dot hack? Want to discuss everything from the original game series to the new .hack//Quantum OVAs? Stop on by and talk about "The World" as we know it.

Translating/Editing panel - Want to learn how translators or editors can make or break a series? We'll talk about the translating and editing process and where they can go wrong.

"See, Skip, Save for Later" panel - We've got an opinion. You might think that show is good, but it's only okay. If you want to see something better, watch this. We'll tell you which shows are good, which ones are okay, and which ones just plain suck. Don't agree? Feel free to voice your opinion.

Marvel - Stan "The Man" Lee has been a powerhouse in entertainment for almost 50 years. Not satified with just being a U.S. hit, he's started to share his influence in Anime. But who is this mysterious mustachioed marvel? Come in as we look at Stanley Lieber's career, paying special attention to foreign works like Heroman, Ultimo, and the Madhouse/Marvel quadrilogy. Excelsior!

Martial Arts - This panel focuses on the depiction of real life martial art styles in anime and manga. Multiple series from several genres will be analyzed, and we will be showcasing multiple videos and pictures for comparison

Who Wants to Be a Voice Actor - Join us for an interactive workshop/discussion/Q&A on what it takes to make it in voiceover. And bring along all those characters in your head – audience members will have a chance to participate in exercises and mock auditions aimed at showing you the ins and outs of voice acting!

Evangelian - Will be a discussion of the Rebuild of Evangelion movies, the alternate universe mangas, as well as the original Neon Genesis Evangelion series.

Weapons - An overview of traditional Japanese weapons.

So You Want To Be... - Interested in making cartooning, comics, and visual storytelling a life pursuit? There's a few things you should know. Join DC from Panda Rage Productions as he covers the use of different resources, methods and approaches to the comicking discipline!
