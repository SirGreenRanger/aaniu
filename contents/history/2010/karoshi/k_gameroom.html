Game Room

Home Karoshi-con Con Registration Game Room Panels Card Game Room

The Karoshi-con Game Room will be featured on the second floor of the Holmes Student Center in the University Suites. The Game Room will feature a multitude of video games for your gaming pleasure during the convention. Whether you want to dance your heart out to DDR or Blue Shell the crap out of a fellow gamer in Mario Kart, the Game Room will be open for you. Game Room staff will be present at all times to provide assistance and enforce rules. A copy of the rules can be found below.

Tournaments


Karoshi-con will be hosting a wide variety of tournaments throughout the course of the convention. As a classic, broadly appealing fighting game, Super Smash Bros. Brawl will be making an appearance as a tournament in the Tournament Room at Karoshi-con. Choose your favorite Nintendo (or Sega) character and duke it out with others for the chance at a prize. Also appearing as a tournament is Marvel vs. Capcom 3, the newest installment in a series of fighting games. Whether you want to destory opponents as Wesker or pound out combos as Spider-man, look forward to this tournament to stretch your fighting game muscles. Lastly, Tekken 6 will be featured. Zafina, Bob, and Miguel Caballero Rojo are all available for your selection in this classic fighting game.

Random Retro Tournament

The main event in the Game Room will be the Random Retro Tournament. Each round will consist of a different game on a random system. Games will range from fighting, shooting, racing, puzzles, and more. Winner of this tournament will have the opportunity to participate in a special Championship Match should he/she decide to return next year.

How the retro tournament works:

  • A game is chosen per round, not per match, using a custom random generation program. Games are chosen on the spot to prevent advanced practice
  • Games may come from, but are not limited to, PS3, XBox 360, Wii, Dreamcast, Gamecube, XBox, PS2, PS1, N64, Saturn, SegaCD, SNES, Genesis, or NES systems.
  • Each match per round shall follow one specific vs. rule-set. Time limits of 5 minutes will be used where applicable
  • Basic controls shall be explained each round, however specific tactics, like character move sets, will not be explained
  • Should both players agree, where applicable, players may look at any in-game control/move lists ONCE per match
  • Tournament officials reserve the right to run the program again before the round's first match should they feel that a certain series/genre has been overdone. However, the officials do not have to re-run should they choose not to

General Tournament Rules:

  • All tournaments will be done in a typical bracketed fashion
  • A "match" will consist of 2-4 players, depending on the game, with the winner(s) moving on
  • A "round" is a series of matches where the players have the same win count, except where seeding is applied, and no one player plays more than once
  • A prize will be awarded to the first place player
  • Any outside distractions, such as physical contact or verbal abuse, will result in the expulsion of the offender
  • Pausing must be announced in advance and agreed to by the other player
  • Any ties will be resolved in a "Sudden Death" match
  • No "boss" characters will be allowed where applicable. Other specific character bans will be done on a game-by-game basis. Game Tournament officials may choose to ban commonly banned characters, but reserve the right not to
  • Names will be called as each match begins. Both players have 15 seconds to announce their presence and move their way up to the chairs before they are considered forfeit. If a player is indisposed, yet has someone to vouch for him/her, the current match will be moved to the end of the round. If either player is not present at the second attempt, he/she will forfeit
  • Controller substitutions will be allowed and control schemes may be changed on a match-by-match basis
  • Due to space limitations, the Game Room will be closed to casual gaming while tournaments are running
