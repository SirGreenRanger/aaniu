Planning Committee

Home Location Constitution Club History Current Shows Events Officers Committee Karoshi-con

The planning committee meets every Sunday starting at 2PM in the front lounge of Founder's Memorial Library on campus. If you are late, go to the second floor and to the Northeast corner meeting room (Room 237). All club members are welcome to attend and help plan new events and show schedules. Any help or ideas you want to provide are more than welcome.
