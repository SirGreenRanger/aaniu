Karoshi-con 2014: Traditional Gaming Room

This room will contain numerous board and card games for all to try out. Enjoy games such as Munchkin, Iron Dragon, and many more! There will be many competitions as well with prizes for the winners! Feel free to grab some friends and head on over; there is open gaming all day!

11:00 AM Magic Skirmish

Come test your Magic the Gathering skills out in this free-to-enter competition! People who place will receive packs of cards to celebrate their victory!

Format: 60-card Casual (All sets are included and legal, NO proxies).

NOT a MTG sanctioned event

1:00 PM Yu-Gi-Oh Tournament

It's time to d-d-d-d-d-duel! Bring your best deck and compete in this tournament to win free cards! There is no entrance fee and the top three placers get to take home some free packs of cards!

NO god-cards allowed and a max of FOUR pieces of Exodia are allowed in any deck (violators will automatically be disqualified).

3:00 PM Mahjong Tournament

Take a chance in this millenia-old game from Asia! This tournament is beginner-friendly and will have trained staff who are willing to teach those willing to learn (there is also a panel before the tournament so if you're itching to start learning be sure to check it out!).
