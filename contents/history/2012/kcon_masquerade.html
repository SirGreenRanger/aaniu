Karoshi-Con 2014: Masquerade

The masquerade is an event for participants to perform costumed skits in front of a live audience and judges. Skits can range anywhere from dancing, fight performances, stand-up comedy, etc. Whether you’re in it for the glory, the prizes, or even if you're doing it for fun, sign up and participate!

Rules and Guidelines:

  1. All Masquerade content must apply to NIU’s Code of Conduct. Keep everything PG-13.
  2. No performances are to exceed 5 minutes.
  3. No messy stage props.
  4. No live steel, fireworks, lasers, smoke machines, or other dangerous props or devices are allowed on stage (or at the convention).
  5. If your skit contains music, please bring either a flashdrive or CD containing an MP3 copy of the song. Don't bring a youtube link, because it could stop to buffer.
