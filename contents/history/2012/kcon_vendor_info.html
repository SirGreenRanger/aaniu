Vendor Information

All of the below information also applies to Artists.

Vendor and artist registration for Karoshi-Con 2014 is open.

The vendor registration fee is $10 and information on how to pay will be given to you if your application is accepted.

The convention runs from 10am to 9pm, but vendors will be asked to clean up an hour or so before the masquerade. Please arrive no earlier than 9am.

Equipment

Power Drops

Power drops will be available if requested when you register, at no additional charge.

Wi-Fi

There is free wi-fi available, but the convention staff can not prevent or predict any downtime that may occur.

Tables

The dimensions of tables available are as follows: 6' x 30"

Rules

The following rules apply to all vendors:

  • Food and beverages for sale must be packaged.
  • No sale of 18+ goods. Sorry, staff and vendors are not allowed to card attendees per HSC policy.
  • No sale of items that may compete with the NIU bookstore. Example: Hoodies. This shouldn't be a problem for most vendors.
  • Any items not listed below must be declared in your registration:
    • Anime, manga, comics, movie, music, game, k-pop, etc. products.
    • Japanese food, snacks, or decorations.
    • Crafts.
  • The staff reserves the right to deny sale of any items.
  • Power drops are limited, up to judgement of the staff.
