Panel Descriptions

Japanese Mahjong 101 11:00 - 12:00 Capitol South

Learn how to play Japanese Mahjong, also known as Riichi Mahjong, a deep and interesting game of strategy and luck. Japanese mahjong is featured in many popular anime, such as "Saki", "Akagi", and "Legendary Gambler Tetsuya". After the panel there will be mahjong in the board and card game room in the Blackhawk Annex.
What Kind of Pokémon are you? 12:10 - 1:10 Capitol North

What Kind of Pokémon are you? How do you do the things you do? Share with me your secrets deep inside…not too deep inside, but we will dig down! We will find your type(s), and discuss how to use your type efficiently, even when you’re not super effective!
Visual novel 101 12:10 - 1:10 Capitol South

Ever wish that you could change what the main character said to that cute childhood friend, or ever been mad that they decided to pursue the green haired loli instead of the tsundere blonde? Visual novels are your answer. Visual novels allow you, the reader, to take part in the story; influencing the plot through dialogue choices, decisions on actions to take, and in some cases, interactive puzzles. I will walk you through the interactive nature of visual novels and even teach you how to create your own!
Prove me Wrong 1:20 - 2:50 Capitol North

Ever been to a panel where the panelists gripe about your favorite shows? Ever wanted to tell them why they're wrong? Well now you can with Prove Me Wrong! And the best part? PRIZES!!!
Using Vocaloid to Create Music 1:20 - 2:50 Capitol South

A demonstration on how to use Vocaloid to create a cover of a karaoke version of an anime song. We will also show how to tune the vocals as best we can using other software.
Name That Tune 3:00 - 4:30 Capitol North

Do you think you can remember the name of that one song from Genshiken? Or maybe what anime Guren no Yumiya is from? Or possibly what video game that background music is from? Then join us for a round of Name That Tune with a nerdy twist! Play as part of one of two teams and compete to name that tune from anime and video games. Seats are limited, so make sure to come early!
Cosplay 101 3:00 - 3:30 Capitol South

Discuss the art of cosplaying, how it came to be, types of cosplay, who you can go to if you can't make a cosplay, and much more. There will be audience participation and cosplay show-and-tell.
Pokémon Contests 3:40 - 5:10 Capitol South

Be the Pokémon coordinator you've always dreamed of being! That's right, Pokémon contests based off of the contests from the popular games and anime. Come prove to everyone just how cute, tough, smart, cool, or beautiful you are, and win prizes for it!
Love is the New Currency!
A Foray into Tanto Cuore		 4:00 - 5:30 Board Game Room

What do Aoi Nanase, KEI, Sato Satoru, and Akio Watanabe (Poyoyon♡Rock) have in common? They provided illustrations to a maid-themed deck building card game called Tanto Cuore. We're providing a free hands-on demonstration of this game but space is limited, so come as early as possible to reserve a spot for yourself.
Whose Line is it Anime 4:40 - 5:40 Capitol North

This is a panel hosted by "Team Gilbert Squad", where the panel is everything is made up and the points don't matter. The winners of the masquerade of 2013 are back, with new friends to have more fun and laughs.
The Anime Dating Game 5:20 - 5:50 Capitol South

Ever watched The Dating Game? Come see it with an anime twist! The eligible bachelor/bachelorette will be timed as they question away three hidden characters. Come see the participants blush, throw some smooth pick up lines, and just have fun while they search for their special someone. A sign-up sheet to participate will be available next to the registration table. Cosplay is a plus!
