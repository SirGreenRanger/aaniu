Karoshi-Con 2014: Panels

Interested in hosting a panel at Karoshi-Con? Want to share your love for the newest anime series, such as Shingeki no Kyojin and Kill la Kill? Want to have a spirited debate about which Doctor was truly the best? Or perhaps you want to host a game pitting competitors against each other in video game knowledge!

If you're interested in hosting a panel at Karoshi-Con 2014, please read the following rules and guidelines.

  1. Panels must have at least a distant relation to the topics of anime, manga, Japan, video games, general nerd culture, etc. Sorry, but guests may not be interested in hearing about your favorite car make and models!
  2. Panels must be submitted with a detailed description of what the panel will consist of, as well as a description that will be printed in the booklet (Edited for length and content by Karoshi-Con staff).
  3. If technology is necessary, please submit with your panel application. (Projector, speakers, stands, etc.)
  4. Not all panels will be accepted! Applications will be reviewed by the Head of Panels before confirmation of addition to the schedule!
  5. Panel submissions must be in timeslots of 30-minute increments. (30 minutes, 1 hour, 1.5 hours, etc.)
  6. Panels will need to end around 10 minutes before the next panel starts.
  7. Time slots cannot be requested. They will be scheduled from 11:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
